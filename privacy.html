Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

The short version: DriveLab collects nothing. Your drives, routes, events, and notes are stored only on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to us. There is no server to send it to.

Location Data

DriveLab uses your location to record the route of a drive while a drive session is running, including in the background so recording continues if you switch apps. Location recording starts when you tap Start Drive and stops when you end the drive.

Your routes are stored in the app's local database on your device. DriveLab never uploads them anywhere.

To label events and drives with street and neighborhood names, and to keep a route on the road through GPS dead zones like tunnels, DriveLab uses Apple's Maps services, the same geocoding built into every iOS maps app. Those requests go to Apple under Apple's privacy policy; DriveLab receives only the resulting names back and stores them on your device.

Microphone and Speech Recognition

When you log an event, DriveLab offers a brief voice note. Audio is transcribed using Apple's on-device speech recognition, and only the resulting text is saved locally with the event. DriveLab does not record or store audio.

Drive Recaps

DriveLab can write a short recap of a drive using Apple Intelligence. The recap is generated entirely on your iPhone by Apple's on-device model; your drive log is never sent anywhere to produce it. Recaps are only available on devices that support Apple Intelligence.

What We Don't Do

  • No accounts. There is nothing to sign up for.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
  • No third-party services or SDKs.
  • No data collection. We have no servers and receive nothing from your device.

Sharing and Export

DriveLab can generate a text summary of a drive, or a markers file for video editors, for you to share. Sharing happens only when you choose to, through the standard iOS share sheet, and you control where it goes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data lives on your device for as long as you keep it. You can delete individual drives in the app, or erase everything with Clear Drive Log in Settings. Deleting the app removes all of its data.

Children

DriveLab is not directed at children and, as a driving tool, is intended for licensed drivers.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version of DriveLab changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised.
